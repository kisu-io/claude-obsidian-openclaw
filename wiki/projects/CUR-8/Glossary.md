Financial / business



Term
Meaning




Capex
Capital expenditure — one-off investment (construction, FF&E, pre-opening)


WC (Working Capital)
Cash buffer to cover operating expenses before revenue ramp


Opex
Operating expense — recurring monthly costs (labor, rent, utilities, etc.)


COGS
Cost of Goods Sold — direct F&B cost of what's sold


EBITDA
Earnings Before Interest, Tax, Depreciation, Amortization


NPAT
Net Profit After Tax


D&A
Depreciation & Amortization


Payback
Time to recover the initial investment from cumulative cash flow


AOV
Average Order Value (used interchangeably with "avg check")


Avg check
Average bill per guest-visit


Seat-turn
Number of times a seat is sold/used in a shift


Realization
% of theoretical maximum revenue actually achieved after losses


Ramp
Period from open to steady-state; typically 3–6 months in F&B



Construction / design



Term
Meaning




BOQ
Bill of Quantities — priced breakdown of all construction line items


DD
Design Development — mid-stage drawings (between SD and CD)


SD
Schematic Design — early concept drawings


CD
Construction Documents — final build drawings


MEP
Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing


FF&E
Furniture, Fixtures & Equipment — loose items, not part of the building


BOH / FOH
Back-of-House / Front-of-House


PM
Project management


NC-20, NC-30
Noise Criterion — acoustic rating; lower = quieter


CFM
Cubic Feet per Minute — airflow measure for HVAC / kitchen hood

Term
Meaning
