Moodboards

Parent: Home · Source: 02_design/moodboards/ (general + F&B) · Related: Detailed-Design, Floor-Layout

Two boards
The design team developed two parallel moodboard tracks:

General moodboard — interior architecture, materials, lighting, atmosphere
F&B moodboard — plating, glassware, menu graphics, beverage presentation

General moodboard



Element
Reference / direction




Materials
Raw concrete walls (PM floors), warm white oak (cafe), brushed brass (bar tops + Sound Lab)


Color
Oxblood, mustard, charcoal grey, off-white, warm timber tones


Lighting
Track + pendant + sconce; no overhead fluorescent; dim down progressively from cafe → bar → Sound Lab


Furniture
Custom banquettes (oxblood velvet, PM); thonet-inspired bentwood (cafe); leather club chairs (Sound Lab)


Acoustic
Wool panels visible in Sound Lab; bass traps in corners; floating floor in Sound Lab


Art
Rotating program — local Vietnamese artists, audio-visual focus


Signage
One wordmark · house serif · brass-on-oak entry plaque


Plantscape
Sparse — fiddle-leaf fig (cafe); rooftop landscaping by specialist



F&B moodboard



Element
Reference / direction




Coffee glassware
Loveramics latte cups + handblown takeaway glass


Cocktail glassware
Coupe (Nick & Nora preferred), highball, rocks; minimal cutting


Plating (cafe)
Stoneware, off-white, single-origin focus on plate styling


Plating (bar)
Smaller portions, layered presentation, oil + acid contrast


Menu graphics
Type-led, no photography; printed on uncoated stock; A5 format


Coasters
Letterpress, monthly rotation


Music sleeves
Vinyl displayed on Sound Lab walls — rotating "now playing"



Mood adjectives (signed off)

Warm — never cold, never sterile
Considered — not minimal, but every choice has reasoning
Audiophile — the speakers are visible, the cables matter
Vietnamese — the materials and palette acknowledge place
Adult — the room respects an adult guest's attention

Explicit anti-references:
- Not Scandinavian-minimal
- Not industrial-loft
- Not Tokyo jazz-kissa pastiche
- Not "Instagram-first" tropical green walls
