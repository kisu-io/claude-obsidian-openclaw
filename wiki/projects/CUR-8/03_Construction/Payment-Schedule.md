Controls

No payment without written milestone sign-off from GM + project manager + relevant engineer (structural/MEP/acoustic).
Change orders issued separately with clear scope + price + impact on timeline; no verbal change orders; budget owner signs.
Retention of 5% is non-negotiable — this is what gets the snag list closed.
Direct payment to the main contractor only; sub-payments are the main contractor's responsibility.
Penalty clause for delay: 1M VND/day past Jun 18 opening, capped at 2% of contract value (~64M).
Bonus clause for early delivery: 0.5M/day pre-Jun 18, capped at 1% (~32M).
