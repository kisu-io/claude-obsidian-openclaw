Contingency
The 8-week schedule has 2.5 days of slack built in (Jun 15–17 buffer before Jun 18 open). That is thin. If any one of the high/medium-impact risks materializes, opening slips into late June.
Recommended: the GM should plan for a soft open Jun 13 (invite-only) and hard open Jun 18, so the buffer is operational rather than purely calendar.
