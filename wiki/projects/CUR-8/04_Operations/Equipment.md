Kitchen lives on Lầu 4 (the set-back top floor, red-box footprint). The DD section still labels this level "Sân Thượng" — drawings need updating. Two operational consequences to keep front-of-mind: (1) deliveries arrive at Trệt and must ride the lift four levels up; (2) plated food rides the lift down to Trệt cafe and Lầu 2 lounge. Service-lift capacity and speed are critical.
