Realization risk

Block B Thu–Sat single-handedly generates 60% of weekly revenue. Concentration risk.
Block A Sun–Wed generates only ~13% of weekly revenue. The cafe carries thin margins; if it underperforms, fixed costs are exposed.
Weather kills rooftop revenue during May–Oct monsoon — assume 30% wet days.
Sound Lab is brand-driving but volume-limited.
