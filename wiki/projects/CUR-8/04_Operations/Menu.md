Margin architecture
Blended target margins:
- Coffee: 78%
- Cafe food: 60%
- Cocktails: 73%
- Beer/wine: 62%
- Bar plates: 62%
- Sound Lab: 75%
Blended ~70% gross margin → 30% COGS → matches model assumption.
Menu discipline rules

No item below 55% margin. Period.
Cocktail menu: 12 signatures + 6 zero-proof + classics on request. Don't expand.
Bar plates: 8 items + 1 rotating. Kitchen capacity caps the menu.
Cafe food: 8 items. Lunch bowls flex daily.
Pricing reviewed quarterly, never reactively to a single bad week.
